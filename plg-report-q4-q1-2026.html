Three Priorities for Q2 2026

Ranked by business impact

01
Fix post-payment retention Critical
77% of Q1 customers who paid are already gone. Launch a 90-day customer success program with proactive check-ins at Day 7, 30, and 60. Study what the 8 active Q1 customers have in common and replicate it.
02
Activate the 54% who never trial High priority
More than half of Q1 signups never started a trial. Add friction-reducing triggers: an in-app prompt on first login, a 3-email onboarding sequence, and show the first AI brand mention within 24 hours of signup.
03
Fix the white-label SEO post CTR Quick win
152,000 impressions in Q1. Only 29 clicks. 0.0% CTR. Rewrite the title and meta description. This is the highest-leverage SEO fix available right now with zero new content required.
